The Phoenix Suns defeated the Memphis Grizzlies, 99 - 97, at Talking Stick Resort Arena on Tuesday night. The Suns (13 - 23) and the Grizzlies (10 - 24) played a very entertaining game that went down to the wire until the final seconds. Phoenix went up by 10 in the first quarter, but Memphis answered right back to the point that they evened things up 76 - 76 heading into the fourth. The Suns had the lead late, but Jarrell Martin evened things up with less than one second left. It seemed things were headed for overtime, but an alley - oop connection between Tyson Chandler and Dragan Bender in the final seconds pulled the Suns up to an improbable win at home. Devin Booker led the way for the Suns in his return from injury, as the shooting guard finished with 32 points, six assists, and five rebounds. Alex Len contributed with 12 points, six boards, and four assists off the bench. T.J. Warren chipped in with 17 points. Tyreke Evans was the best player for the Grizzlies, as the versatile combo guard once again dazzled thanks to 25 points, five rebounds, and five assists. Marc Gasol contributed on both ends of the court, as he ended with 18 points, six rebounds, and four blocks. James Ennis III added 11 points and five boards with two assists off the bench.
