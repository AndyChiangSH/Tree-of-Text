The Cleveland Cavaliers defeated the visiting Memphis Grizzlies, 112 - 107, at Quicken Loans Arena on Saturday evening. The Cavs got out to a great start at home, with a 37 - 24 lead after one quarter. The Grizzlies pushed back in the second, where they outscored the Cavs by six, to cut the deficit to seven by halftime. The Grizzlies carried the momentum into the third quarter, where they outscored the Cavs by nine, to take a two - point lead into the final frame. The Cavs were not done, as they started the fourth on a 6 - 1 run, to reclaim the lead. The score went back and forth, until the Cavs created some separation in the final minutes with a couple of big plays from Collin Sexton and Cedi Osman. The Cavs went on to secure their second straight win, while the Grizzlies dropped their fourth in a row. Kevin Love led the Cavs with 32 points, 12 rebounds, one steal and one block, in 25 minutes. Collin Sexton followed up with 20 points, six rebounds, five assists and two steals, while Ante Zizic tallied 18 points, seven rebounds and one assist. The Cavs shot 44 percent from the field, including 16 - of - 42 from deep. Meanwhile, Jonas Valanciunas led the Grizzlies with 25 points, 11 rebounds, two blocks and one assist, in 29 minutes. Mike Conley tallied 18 points, five rebounds, three steals and two assists, while Joakim Noah added 16 points, seven rebounds, four assists, two blocks and a steal, in 32 minutes off the bench. The Grizzlies (23 - 38) will look to bounce back as they play host to the Lakers on Monday. The Cavs (14 - 46) play host to the Trail Blazers on Monday.
