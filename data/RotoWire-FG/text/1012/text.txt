The Los Angeles Clippers took down the visiting Phoenix Suns, 115 - 99, on Wednesday evening. It was a battle of first meets last on Wednesday, as the top - ranked Clippers (14 - 6) hosted the 15th - place Suns (4 - 17). The game went accordingly, as LA earned a blowout win to maintain its first - place standing. With 11 made three - pointers and 18 offensive rebounds, the Clippers were able to overcome some struggles with ball management. It was an easy win for the team despite committing 21 turnovers on the night. LA has now won eight of its last nine games and is showing no signs of slowing down. Danilo Gallinari led the Clippers with 28 points and grabbed 10 rebounds in the win. His fellow forward, Tobias Harris, added another 10 rebounds to go with 18 points on the night. Big man Boban Marjanovic, in just 19 minutes on the court, secured 12 rebounds, including eight on the offensive end. All three stellar frontcourt performances contributed to LA's comfortable victory. Phoenix, on the other hand, struggled mightily on the offensive end. The team shot just 31 percent from three - point range and 43 percent overall. Devin Booker led the team with 23 points. It was an off night for rookie big man DeAndre Ayton, who committed five fouls and finished with just four points in 30 minutes of play. The Clippers visit the Kings on Thursday, while Phoenix will host the Orlando Magic on Friday.
