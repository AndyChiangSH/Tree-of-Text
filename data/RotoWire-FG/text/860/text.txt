The Portland Trail Blazers defeated the visiting San Antonio Spurs, 111 - 110, at Moda Center on Sunday evening. This game was a thriller right from the start, as the two teams played to a tie through the first quarter. The second quarter was tight as well but the Spurs pulled away towards the end to take a 53 - 48 lead at halftime. The Trail Blazers took their turn in the third quarter, and fought back to tie it up at 80 heading into the fourth. The fourth quarter was heated, with the Spurs pulling ahead, before Portland pushed back to take a seven - point lead with 5:30 to go. The drama was no where near finished though, as the Spurs took one last lead, up 110 - 109 with 13 seconds left, setting the stage for C.J. McCollum to bury the game - winning bucket with five seconds left. The Spurs had time for a final shot but it fell short, leading to the Trail Blazers' second straight win. C.J. McCollum led the way for Portland with 25 points on 11 - of - 24 shooting, in 38 minutes. Jusuf Nurkic added 17 points and 13 rebounds, while Maurice Harkless tallied 19 points on 7 - of - 10 shooting. Evan Turner finished with eight points on 4 - of - 9 shooting, in 23 minutes, and Shabazz Napier added 15 points, seven assists and five rebounds. The Blazers shot 52 percent from the field, including 8 - of - 23 from long range. Meanwhile, LaMarcus Aldridge led the Spurs with 30 points and 14 rebounds, in 34 minutes. Pau Gasol tallied 16 points. nine rebounds, five assists and two steals. Manu Ginobili came up big with 26 points on 9 - of - 16 shooting, in 27 minutes off the bench. The Spurs shot 49 percent from the field, including 11 - of - 23 from long range. The Spurs (27 - 14) will be right back in action as they take on the Kings in Sacramento on Monday. The Trail Blazers (21 - 18) head to OKC to take on the Thunder on Tuesday.
