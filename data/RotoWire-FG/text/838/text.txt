The New Orleans Pelicans defeated the host Miami Heat, 109 - 94, at American Airlines Arena on Saturday. Miami are dealing with as many injuries as anyone right now, as they simply couldn't match up with this potent New Orleans roster in this game. The middle quarters were key, with New Orleans outscoring Miami, 56 - 41, as the Pelicans held the lead for the entirety of the second half. New Orleans were lights - out, as they shot 53 percent from the field and 60 percent from three - point range. Miami shot just 42 percent from the field and 31 percent from long range. The Pelicans (17 - 16) were led by Anthony Davis, as he tallied 16 points, six rebounds, three assists and four blocks. DeMarcus Cousins finished with 16 points, seven rebounds, eight assists, two steals and three blocks. Jrue Holiday collected 13 points, six rebounds and four assists. Ian Clark was huge off the bench, as he dropped 19 points on 5 - of - 6 from three - point range. The Heat (17 - 16) were led by Tyler Johnson, as he amassed 20 points, three rebounds and two assists. Goran Dragic totaled 13 points, four rebounds and two assists. Josh Richardson accrued 12 points, four rebounds and two assists. On deck for Miami is a home matchup versus the Orlando Magic on Tuesday. New Orleans will be off until Wednesday, as they host the Brooklyn Nets.
