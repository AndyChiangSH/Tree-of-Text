The New Orleans Pelicans defeated the Los Angeles Clippers, 116 - 109, at Smoothie King Center on Tuesday night. The Pelicans (30) and The Clippers (2 - 2) played a back - and - forth game, but in the end the hosts came through and used a big second half to keep their undefeated streak alive. New Orleans jumped to a 27 - 26 lead after the first quarter, but the Clippers stormed back with a 36 - 32 second quarter that lifted them to a 62 - 59 lead at the break. However, the Pelicans didn't want to lose their undefeated record and flipped the script in the second half: a 32 - 22 third quarter pushed them ahead into the final 12 minutes, and they closed things out with a 25 - 25 fourth quarter that secured them a seven - point win while keeping their winning run alive to start the 2018 - 19 season. Anthony Davis led the way for the Pelicans with 34 points and 13 rebounds, but he wasn't the only Pelican who posted a double - double since Nikola Mirotic contributed with 18 points and 12 rebounds. Elfrid Payton had 20 points, six rebounds and six assists, while E'Twaun Moore added 14 points. Julius Randle chipped in with 18 points and five rebounds in 22 minutes off the bench. Tobias Harris was the best player for Clippers since he finished with 26 points and nine rebounds, while Patrick Beverley came close to a triple - double since he contributed with 12 points, 10 rebounds and eight assists. Danilo Gallinari had 24 points with six rebounds, while Lou Williams (17 points) and Montrezl Harrell (12 points, six rebounds) contributed off the bench.
