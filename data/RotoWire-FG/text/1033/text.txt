The Miami Heat defeated the host Orlando Magic, 115 - 91, at Amway Center on Sunday evening. The Heat got out to a great start on the road by outscoring the Magic by 11 in the first quarter. The Magic tightened up in the second and cut the deficit to nine by halftime. However, the visitors showed no mercy, as they came storming out in the third to outscore the Magic by 10 in the quarter and take a 19 - point lead into the final frame. The Magic had no answer as their state rivals cruised to the 24 - point win. This marks the fifth straight win for the Heat, and the third loss in a row for the Magic. The Heat had six players score in double digits, led by Tyler Johnson, who finished with 25 points, 20 of which he scored in the third quarter alone. Justise Winslow added 22 points, six rebounds, five assists and four steals, while Josh Richardson tallied 15 points, 10 rebounds, four assists and two steals. The Heat shot 51 percent from the field, including 16 - of - 31 from long range. Meanwhile, Evan Fournier led the Magic with 17 points, six rebounds, four assists and a steal, in 26 minutes. Aaron Gordon added 14 points, six rebounds, four assists, one steal and a block, while the only other player to score in double digits for Orlando was Jonathan Simmons, with 11 points. The Magic (14 - 18) will look to bounce back as they play host to the Suns on Wednesday. The Heat (16 - 16) play host to the Raptors on Wednesday.
