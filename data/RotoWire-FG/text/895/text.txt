The Utah Jazz defeated the Memphis Grizzlies, 92 - 88, at FedEx Forum on Wednesday evening. Both teams started out slow. The Jazz took an eight point lead mid way though the first and hung on to lead 21 - 16 heading into the second. The Grizzlies battled back in the second and tied it up at 28 with six minutes left in the quarter, but the Jazz pulled away and settled on a 44 - 39 lead by halftime. The Jazz and Grizzlies played to a tie though the third, so that the Jazz took a five - point lead into the fourth. The Jazz expanded their lead to 11, with 7:30 left in the fourth and did not look back from there, en route to their seventh straight win. Ricky Rubio led the way for the Jazz with 29 points on 8 - of - 16 shooting, eight rebounds, three assists and three steals, in 28 minutes of action. Rodney Hood followed up with 18 points on 5 - of - 12 shooting, while Rudy Gobert tallied eight points, 12 rebounds, two assists and two blocks. The Jazz shot 42 percent from the field, including 6 - of - 22 from long range. Meanwhile, Andrew Harrison was the high - point man for the Grizzlies, with 23 points on 9 - of - 15 shooting, in 35 minutes. Marc Gasol followed up with 20 points on 7 - of - 20 shooting, six rebounds, five assists, three blocks and one steal, in 36 minutes. Dillon Brooks and JaMychal Green chipped in 11 points each. The Grizzlies shot 42 percent from the field, including 8 - of - 23 from long range. The Grizzlies (18 - 36) will look to end a five game losing streak, as they take on the Thunder in OKC on Sunday. The Jazz (26 - 28) play host to the Hornets on Friday.
