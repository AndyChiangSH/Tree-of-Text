The Atlanta Hawks defeated the visiting Dallas Mavericks, 112 - 107, at Philips Arena on Saturday evening. The teams fought to a tie through the first quarter. The second quarter stayed tight as well, with neither team taking a lead greater than a couple of possessions, with the Mavericks leading 53 - 48 at halftime. The Hawks started out the third with a 7 - 2 run to tie it up at 55. They continued to apply pressure and led for most of the third before the Mavericks rallied back to take a one - point, 80 - 79, lead heading into the fourth. Thing stayed close for most of the fourth, with the Mavs leading by two with 4:40 to go. However, the Hawks turned it up down the stretch, taking a slight edge and maintaining it for the final minutes. Dennis Schroder led the way for the Hawks, with 33 points on 13 - of - 22 shooting, seven assists, three rebounds and one steal, in 35 minutes. Ersan Ilyasova followed up with 21 points, seven rebounds, two assists, one steal and a block, while John Collins chipped in 13 points, four rebounds and two assists, in 20 minutes off the bench. The Hawks shot 57 percent from the field, including 10 - of - 24 from long range. Meanwhile, Harrison Barnes led Dallas with 22 points on 10 - of - 21 shooting, in 34 minutes. J.J. Barea came up large off the bench, with 15 points, 12 assists, three steals and two rebounds, in 29 minutes. Dirk Nowitzki finished with 18 points, six rebounds, four assists and one block. The Mavericks shot 51 percent from the field, including 9 - of - 33 from distance. The Mavs (9 - 25) play host to the Raptors on Boxing Day. The Hawks (8 - 25) play host to the Wizards on Wednesday.
