The Los Angeles Clippers defeated the host Los Angeles Lakers, 118 - 107, at Staples Center on Friday evening. The Clippers got out to a strong start as they led 28 - 23 after on quarter. The Lakers turned the tables in the second, as they outscored the Clippers by eight in the quarter, to take a three - point lead by halftime. However, the Clippers came out strong in the second half and regained control through the third, where they outscored the Lakers by five, to take an 82 - 80 lead into the final frame. They kept the pressure on through the fourth and outscored the Lakers by nine in the quarter, to secure their second straight win. This marked the second straight loss for the Lakers as they carry on without LeBron James (groin). Lou Williams led the Clippers with 36 points, seven rebounds and three assists, in 30 minutes off the bench. Danilo Gallinari finished with 19 points, 10 rebounds, three assists, one steal and one block, while Tobias Harris tallied nine points, 10 rebounds, one assist and one steal. The Clippers shot 44 percent from the field, including 6 - of - 19 from long range. Meanwhile, Kyle Kuzma led the Lakers with 24 points, eight rebounds and five assists, in 41 minutes. Lonzo Ball tallied 19 points, six assists, five rebounds and one steal, while Brandon Ingram added 17 points and seven rebounds. The Lakers shot 41 percent from the field, including 11 - of - 30 from long range. The Lakers (20 - 16) will look to bounce back as they play host to the Kings on Sunday. The Clippers (21 - 14) play host to the Spurs on Saturday.
