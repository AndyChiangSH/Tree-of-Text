The New Orleans Pelicans defeated the visiting Memphis Grizzlies, 123 - 95, at Smoothie King Center on Wednesday evening. The Pelicans got out to a good start right from the jump, as they rushed out to a 100 lead, and hung on to lead 32 - 23 by the end of the first quarter. The Grizzlies made a push early in the second, making it a three - point game, but the Pelicans quickly took back the momentum and built a double - digit lead. The Pelicans led for the rest of the quarter and took a 65 - 53 lead into halftime. The Grizzlies came totally undone in the third, as the Pelicans extended their lead to 21, just four minutes into the quarter. There was not much competition from that point on as the Pelicans cruised to a much - needed win, ending a four - game losing streak. E'Twaun Moore led the Pelicans with 30 points, four assists, three steals, one rebound and one block, in 29 minutes. Anthony Davis tallied 28 points, 12 rebounds, three blocks, two steals and two assists, while Nikola Mirotic added 25 points, seven rebounds and one block. The Pelicans shot 52 percent from the field, including 16 - of - 36 from long range. Meanwhile, MarShon Brooks led Memphis with 25 points, seven assists, four rebounds and two steals, in 36 minutes off the bench. Ivan Rabb added 16 points and seven rebounds, off the bench, while Jarell Martin finished with 15 points, five rebounds and one assist. The Grizzlies shot 40 percent from the field, including 10 - of - 32 from long range. The Grizzlies (21 - 57) play host to the Kings on Friday. The Pelicans (44 - 34) take on the Suns in Phoenix on Friday.
