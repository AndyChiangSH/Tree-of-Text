The Miami Heat defeated the Indiana Pacers, 114 - 106, at Bankers Life Fieldhouse on Wednesday evening. The Heat (24 - 17) delivered another impressive performance, and remain one of the hottest teams in the NBA at the moment since they are currently riding a six - game winning streak. Indiana (21 - 10) tried to fight back after they quickly fell to a 12 - point hole after the first quarter and they made things complicated at times for Miami, but the Heat managed to hold on to their advantage and secured another win that continues their surge in the Eastern Conference standings. Goran Dragic was the best player for the Heat, as the Slovenian point guard finished the game with 20 points, nine assists and three rebounds. Hassan Whiteside posted a strong double - double of 16 points, 15 rebounds, and four blocks. Josh Richardson chipped in with 14 points, and Kelly Olynyk added 11 of his own. The bench produced as well, as the trio of Wayne Ellington, Bam Adebayo and Tyler Johnson all contributed with 15 points. Victor Oladipo once again led the way for the Pacers offensively since he contributed with 26 points, seven rebounds, four assists, and three steals. Domantas Sabonis contributed with 18 points, and Thaddeus Young added 12 points with nine rebounds. Bojan Bogdanovic had 15 points and six rebounds, and Lance Stephenson added 15 points coming off the bench.
