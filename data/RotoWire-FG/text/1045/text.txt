The Philadelphia 76ers defeated the host Phoenix Suns, 132 - 127, at Talking Stick Resort Arena on Wednesday evening. The Sixers got out to a great start on the road, as they poured in 44 points in the first quarter and took a 44 - 32 lead into the second. They kept it up in the second, where they outscored the Suns by 11, to take a 72 - 49 lead into halftime. The Suns came out with a purpose in the third and managed to outscore the visitors by six in the quarter, to cut the deficit to 17 by the start of the fourth. They kept up the effort in the fourth quarter and managed to make it a four - point game with just 13 seconds left. However, the Sixers hung on to earn their second straight win. Joel Embiid led the 76ers with a monstrous performance, tallying 42 points, 18 rebounds, three steals, two blocks and two assists, in 36 minutes. Ben Simmons followed up with 29 points, six assists, three rebounds and one block, while J.J. Redick added 27 points, four rebounds, four assists and two steals. The Sixers shot 54 percent from the field, including 11 - of - 24 from long range. Meanwhile, Devin Booker led the Suns with 37 points, eight assists and two rebounds, in 35 minutes. Deandre Ayton tallied 18 points, 11 rebounds, two assists and two steals, while Josh Jackson chipped in 16 points, six rebounds, four assists, a steal and a block, in 26 minutes off the bench. The Suns shot 45 percent from the field, including 11 - of - 34 from long range. The Suns (9 - 30) will look to avoid a fifth straight loss as they play host to the Clippers on Friday. The 76ers (25 - 14) play host to the Mavericks on Saturday.
