On July 2, 2022, at the PETRONAS Malaysia Open, Thailand's Ratchanok Intanon faced China's Wang Zhiyi in the women's singles semifinals. Intanon secured her place in the final with a straight-sets victory, winning 21-8, 21-18. 

In the first game, Intanon dominated, taking an early lead and maintaining control to win 21-8. The second game was more competitive, with Wang challenging Intanon's defense. However, Intanon's experience prevailed, and she closed the game at 21-18, clinching the match.

This win advanced Intanon to the finals, where she competed against China's Chen Yufei.
