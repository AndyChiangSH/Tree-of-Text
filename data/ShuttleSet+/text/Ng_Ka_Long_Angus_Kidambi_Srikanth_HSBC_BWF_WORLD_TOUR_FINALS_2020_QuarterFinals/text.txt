Second Game: Kidambi Srikanth made significant adjustments in the second game, showing resilience and determination. He fought back and managed to level the score, ultimately winning the game 21-18. This victory reinvigorated his chances in the match and showcased his ability to compete under pressure.

Deciding Game: The final game was a thrilling battle, with both players exchanging points in a tense atmosphere.
