## PV Sindhu vs An Seyoung

India's PV Sindhu is up against local star An Seyoung. The two stars are welcomed by a loud cheer from the fans.

An Seyoung to serve.

## An Seyoung flying ahead

An easy first point for An Seyoung to start the proceedings and a net shot from PV Sindhu.

A long rally and the South Korean saves a point from out of nowhere and also wins the point. 3-0 to An Seyoung.

Sindhu needs to control the power on her shots as two have already gone out of bounds.

An Seyoung comes up with two full stretch saves in the 32-shot rally! She makes it 7-1. The South Korean looks very confident.

The Indian ace pockets three straight points. Looks like Sindhu is finding her groove. 9-6 now.

An Seyoung is not letting Sindhu find her rhythm and goes into the break with a five-point lead.

Score: An Seyoung 11-6 PV Sindhu

## An Seyoung wins opener

The South Korean star has an array of shots in her book and she is using them all against Sindhu.

Two long shots from PV Sindhu and the lead goes up to seven points.

The Indian gets two points after long rallies but Seyoung is predicting the shots well. It’s still 16-10.

Just when it looks like Sindhu is coming back, An Seyoung puts a brake on it.

A couple of long shots from SIndhu, as the young South Korean is only one point away from winning the opening game.

Sindhu saves two game points but Seyoung sends a strong smash to pocket the opener 21-14.

## A close second game

A much better start from Sindhu in the second game as she wins the first three points.

Three wide returns from Sindhu give An her first points of the game and level the scores. The Indian needs to stop the unforced errors.

An improved performance from Sindhu but An Seyoung is not letting this go easily. It’s been a tight game so far, tied at 6-6.

The exchange of points continues and An Seyoung goes up 11-9 into the break.

## An Seyoung beats PV Sindhu

What reflexes from An Seyoung! The home girl is answering all of Sindhu’s shots with aplomb.

However, Sindhu has a period of brilliance and she reduces the lead to 14-13.

Now An Seyoung puts a stop to Sindhu’s advances and pockets two straight points.

The Indian mishits an easy smash but recovers in the next point. Seyoung still leads 18-16.

An Seyoung nets a drive but inches closer to the finish line with a searing winner to the right of Sindhu.

Another body shot from Seyoung to set up the match point and a smash to sail into the final of the Korea Open. What a player she is! She has now won all four matches against Sindhu.

Score: An Seyoung 21-14, 21-17 PV Sindhu
