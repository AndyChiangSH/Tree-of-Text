Yamaguchi vs. An Se-young in Uber Cup Semifinals:

In the semifinals of the TotalEnergies BWF Uber Cup Finals 2022 , Akane Yamaguchi faced An Se-young on May 13, 2022. An Se-young secured a crucial victory for South Korea Yamaguchi with scores of 21-19, 18-21, 21-18 . This match was pivotal as it contributed to South Korea's overall win against Japan, leading to a 3-0 victory in the tie.
